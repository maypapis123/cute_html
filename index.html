Delicious Vanilla Cake Recipe

Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 3 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt; gradually stir into the creamed mixture alternately with the milk.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  7. Allow to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
  8. Enjoy your delicious vanilla cake!
